Support

Help with GymSync, account questions, and how to reach us.

Common topics

  • Sign-in issues: Confirm you’re using the same method (email, Apple, or Google) you used to create your account. Reset your password from the sign-in screen if you use email.
  • Photos not saving: Check that GymSync has camera or photo library permission in your device settings.
  • Notifications: Reminders depend on system permissions. Enable notifications for GymSync in your device settings if you want alerts.
  • Data & privacy: See our Privacy Policy for what we collect and how to exercise your choices.

Contact us

For privacy requests, account help, or general feedback, email us and include your platform (iOS/Android) and a short description of the issue.
